## About Datayug Society

**Datayug Society** is a non-profit organization founded in 2023, based in **Shillong, Meghalaya, India**. We are dedicated to strengthening digital literacy and cyber awareness by educating individuals and communities on the safe, responsible, and confident use of technology.

Through workshops, awareness programs, and community-driven initiatives, we help people across all age groups protect themselves from cyber threats and bridge the digital divide in an increasingly connected world.

### Our Mission
- **Digital Inclusion & Community Growth** — Promoting equal access to technology while fostering community-driven development
- **Education & Empowerment** — Enabling individuals through knowledge, skills, and digital literacy
- **Online Safety, Integrity & Accountability** — Encouraging responsible digital behavior built on trust, ethics, and security

- **Mar – Oct 2025** — Hosted 5,000+ students across rural and urban schools for digital literacy workshops
- **Sep 2024** — Collaboration with Cyber Wing, Meghalaya Police; joint session at Synod College on tracking cyber crimes
- **Aug – Dec 2024** — Cyberbullying & social media impact workshops at 5 schools in Shillong
- **May 2024** — Internship drive at National Law University (NLU) Meghalaya

## How We Help Cyber Crime Victims

1. **Initial Support** — We act as a bridge between victims and the cyber cell, simplifying the process of seeking help
2. **Complaint Assistance** — We guide victims through the required steps to properly lodge complaints
3. **Ongoing Guidance** — A Datayug representative supports the victim throughout the process, ensuring smooth communication and resolution
