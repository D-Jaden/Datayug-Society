Meet Our Founder

Menoka Naug Mahato is the Founder and President of Datayug Society, an organization committed to promoting cyber awareness and digital literacy. With professional experience in sales and marketing, she developed strong communication, leadership, and community engagement skills. Her transition into social impact was inspired by a real-life incident involving a close acquaintance who became a victim of cyber fraud.

Witnessing the emotional distress and legal complications associated with cybercrime cases and understanding how difficult it can be for victims to receive timely support highlighted a critical gap in digital awareness at the grassroots level. Recognizing that prevention through education is more powerful than reaction after damage, she established Datayug Society to create an accessible platform where individuals can learn about cyber safety, digital responsibility, and online protection without hesitation.

Under her leadership, the organization works toward building a digitally informed and cyber-aware society through structured awareness programs and community outreach initiatives.
