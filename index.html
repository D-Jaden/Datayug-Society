WHO ARE WE ?

Datayug Society is a non-profit organization founded in 2023 based in Shillong, Meghalaya. We work to strengthen digital literacy and cyber awareness by educating individuals and communities on safe, responsible, and confident use of technology.


Through workshops, awareness programs, and community-driven initiatives, we help people across all age groups protect themselves from cyber threats and bridge the digital divide in an increasingly connected world.

Our Values

Digital Inclusion & Community Growth

Promoting equal access to technology while fostering community-driven development

Education & Empowerment

Enabling individuals through knowledge, skills, and digital literacy.

Online Safety, Integrity & Accountability

Encouraging responsible digital behavior built on trust, ethics, and security.

How Does Datayug Help Cyber Crime Victims?

1

Initial Support

Datayug acts as a bridge between cyber crime victims and the cyber cell, simplifying the process of seeking help and guidance.
2

Complaint Assistance

It assists victims in properly lodging complaints by guiding them through the required steps and procedures.
3

Ongoing Guidance

A Datayug representative supports the victim throughout the process, ensuring smooth communication and resolution with the cyber cell.
